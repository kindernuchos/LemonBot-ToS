# LemonBot-ToS
These Terms of Service outline the rules and restrictions for using LemonBot. By using the bot, you agree to follow all guidelines and understand that violations may result in punishments, restrictions, or permanent loss of access.


#**1**: **No abusing economy commands.**

- Do not spam or abuse commands such as L?spawnlemon, or any other command that generates lemons, rewards, XP, or items unfairly.

#**2**: **No exploiting glitches, dupes, or bugs.**

- Using bugs, errors, unintended mechanics, dupes, macros, autoclickers, scripts, or exploits to gain advantages is strictly prohibited.

- Report bugs in bug-reports instead of abusing them.

- You may be notified about known bugs that should not be abused.

#**3**: **No alt account farming.**

- Using alternate accounts to farm lemons, transfer wealth, bypass cooldowns, boost gangs, complete quests, manipulate raids, or abuse rewards is not allowed.

#**4**: **No scams or fake trades.**

- Scamming users through the market, auctions, misleading gang deals, fake giveaways, or dishonest economy interactions is forbidden.

#**5**: **No leaderboard manipulation.**

- Artificially boosting stats, streaks, rankings, battle pass progress, achievements, stock value, or gang wealth through unfair methods is not allowed.

#**6**: **No abuse of gangs, raids, or heists.**

- Exploiting gang wars, heists, boss raids, or gang systems to duplicate rewards or avoid intended risks is prohibited.

#**7**: **No automation.**

- Bots, macros, auto-typers, scripts, or third-party software used to automate commands or gameplay are forbidden.

#**8**: **Staff may remove exploited progress.**

- Any lemons, items, pets, upgrades, stocks, titles, factories, or progress obtained unfairly may be removed without warning.
